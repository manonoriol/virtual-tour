our story

Gallery360 is a Paris based creative studio conceiving 360° and 3D virtual visits. Museums, galleries, showrooms, fashion shows, trade shows - give your worldwide clients and leads the opportunity to come again at your show. We have created a virtual visit solution that allows you to present a virtual visit of your space on your website through a unique interactive experience : tell actual stories with multifunctional hotspots and clickable objects that your audience discovers when walking through the tour. With a cross-cultural and limitless approach of design, our work covers many different fields including contemporary art, design and fashion for both self-initiated and commissioned projects.
