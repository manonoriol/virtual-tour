Gallery360 is a Paris based creative studio conceiving 360° and 3D virtual visits. We have created a solution that allows you to present a virtual visit of your space on your website, through a unique and interactive experience. Our tool enables you to tell stories with multifunctional hotspots and clickable objects. With a cross-cultural and limitless approach of design, our work covers many different fields including contemporary art, design and fashion for both self-initiated and commissioned projects.
